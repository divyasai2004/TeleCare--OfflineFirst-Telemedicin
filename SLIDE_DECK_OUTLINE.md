# 🎨 Design Guidelines

## Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Green (#1a5c45) - Healthcare, trust
- **Secondary:** Cream (#f7f3ee) - Warmth, accessibility
- **Accent:** Amber (#b45309) - Urgency, attention
- **Text:** Dark gray (#1a202c) - Readability

## Typography
- **Headings:** Lora (serif) - Warmth, trust
- **Body:** DM Sans (sans-serif) - Modern, readable

## Visual Elements
- Use real screenshots from your app
- Include icons for each feature
- Use infographics for statistics
- Show before/after comparisons
- Include user journey diagrams

## Slide 2 (Problem)
"Let me paint the picture: Nabha Civil Hospital serves 173 villages with only 11 doctors. That's less than 50% capacity. Patients travel 30-50 km, lose entire days of work, spend ₹500-1000 just in lost wages. And 69% have unreliable internet. This is the reality of rural healthcare."

"This is our crown jewel - the symptom checker. When online, it uses AI. When offline, it uses intelligent rules. Either way, it works. A farmer at 2 AM with chest pain gets flagged as urgent immediately. This saves lives."

## Slide 10 (Technical Innovation)
"We didn't build a web app and make it mobile-friendly. We built for rural India from day one. Offline-first means healthcare never stops. 2G optimization means it works for 69% of users. This is intentional design."
