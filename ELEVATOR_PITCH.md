---

## The Solution (40 seconds)
"**CareConnect** - An offline-first telemedicine platform that works on 2G networks.

**Core Features:**
1. **AI Symptom Checker** - Works offline, flags emergencies immediately
2. **Chat Consultations** - Connect with doctors without leaving home
3. **Digital Health Records** - Complete medical history, always accessible
4. **Medicine Finder** - Know where medicines are available before traveling
5. **Emergency Services** - One-tap ambulance, nearest hospital finder

**The Magic:** Our symptom checker works **completely offline** using intelligent rule-based algorithms. When online, AI enhances it. **Healthcare never stops.**"

---

## Impact (30 seconds)
"**Immediate Impact:**
- Saves patients ₹500-1000 per consultation (lost wages)
- Reduces travel time by 80%
- Enables 11 doctors to serve more patients efficiently
- Provides 24/7 symptom guidance

**Real Story:** That farmer at 2 AM? He checks symptoms offline, gets flagged as urgent, calls 108 ambulance, and reaches hospital in time. **We saved a life.**"
